GDPR notes:

gdpr lays down the rules for data protection with regard to procession personal data

article 2 material scope: automated, partially automated and manually processed data with the scope 
	inside the EU

article 3 territorial scope:  applies to the processing of personal data of data subjects who are in 
	the Union by a controller or processor not established in the Union processing activities are
	 related to services, goods, monitoring behavior as far as their behaviour is in the EU
